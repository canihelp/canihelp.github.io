About

How can I help is just as it sounds. Have any questions? Stuck on a problem? Looking to speak to someone? Just reach out.

In an effort to bringing something to this world, I want to help people in any way I can. Feel free to read the Goal section below to learn about the inspiration behind this project.

Looking forward to all the requests!

Goal

Benjamin Franklin used to go to bed every night and ask himself, “What good have I done today?” Lately, I have been asking myself the same question. Besides working on projects at work that is helping people, I realized that I wasn’t doing that much more outside of it. Sure, I help my family and friends, but that’s just always been the case. What have I done to help in this world? I am not trying to be pessimistic, yet this realization has led me to this project: How can I help?

There is a nice quote that I can’t quite seem to forget: “It’s not what the world holds for you; it’s what you bring to it.” This project is about just that. What am I bringing to the world? Thinking hard on my skills and my interest, I realized that the best thing I can offer is my time and commitment to helping others. How? By any means within my control.

I’d like to ask anyone who comes across this website to feel free to reach out to me and ask any questions or concerns you have; any particular requests you have either regarding personal development or career growth; or if you just want to say hello. This is a way for me to offer my help to anyone who wants it. My goal is to eventually share my stories and bring others into this project to help as many people as we can.
